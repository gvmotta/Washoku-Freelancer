Introduction to Culinary School

Welcome to the renowned Japanese Culinary Innovation School in Tokyo, where culinary excellence meets the art of innovation. We are a renowned institute recognized for providing exceptional courses that attract chefs looking for the highest quality and creativity.

With instructors who have extensive experience in Michelin-starred restaurants, we offer a comprehensive curriculum that covers everything from the classic fundamentals to the latest trends in modern Japanese cuisine. When you join our school, you will have the opportunity to explore premium ingredients, hone your presentation skills, and develop a deep understanding of the harmony of flavors characteristic of Japanese cuisine.

Join us on this unique and transformative journey, where tradition and innovation meet to create extraordinary dishes.

Dia 8 - 14:

  • Manuseio de 10 tipos de peixes diferentes
  • Molde do rolo de sushi
  • Palestra em operações de restaurantes

Dia 15 - 20:

  • Manuseio de 5 tipos de peixes diferentes
  • Pratos japoneses básicos com peixe
  • Visita ao mercado de peixe (sujeito a disponibilidade)
